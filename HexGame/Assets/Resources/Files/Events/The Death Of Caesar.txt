And therefore he was stabbed with twenty three wounds, without bringing fourth a word, but merely a groan at the first hit, although some recount that when Marcus Brutus rushed, he said in Greek, "You too, my child?"

- Gaius Suetonius Tranquillus, The twelve Caesars
